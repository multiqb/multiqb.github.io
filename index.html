your Qb

The equipment is recommended for use in offices to provide wired and wireless connection to the Internet via LTE / WIFI networks, with the ability to play media content, telecast and make video call.

  • For business

Wireless

To provide the last mile link for clients in hard-to-reach areas providers can establish GRE tunnels over LTE 4G networks to deliver static IP addresses to client network equipment.
This allows servicing customers located in suburban areas with no land cable connection, and quickly connecting ATMs, POS terminals and payment terminals without incurring significant costs for land connection.

Reliable

To reserve last mile connection for clients in unstable zones, providers can use dynamic routing protocols such as BGP, OSPF, RIP, IS-IS and PIM over LTE 4G with GRE tunnels to deliver static IP addresses to client network equipment.
This makes it possible to significantly improve the fault tolerance of the Internet channel of the end user under temporary force majeure circumstances, increase the quality of customer service, and make time for engineers to eliminate Internet connection problems imperceptibly for customers.

All in One

One device is all it takes to connect customers to TV, Internet and telephony services providers while maintaining flexibility and ease of installation.
This significantly reduces the cost of equipment and time of customer service. In the end it positively affects the work of technical support staff.

Mobile Office
